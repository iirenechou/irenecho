General Information
  • Bring your ID if you are a first-time voter.
  • Voting is open to all registered voters in Pennsylvania.
  • You must be at least 18 years old by Election Day to vote.
  • You can vote in person at your assigned polling place or by mail-in/absentee ballot.
  • Assistance is available for voters with disabilities or those needing language support.
  • You can check your voter registration status online before Election Day.
  • You must vote at your assigned polling place if voting in person.

  • Mail-in voting is available to all voters, no excuse needed.
  • Absentee voting requires a valid reason, such as being out of town or having a disability.
  • Mail-in and absentee ballots must be requested by a certain deadline before Election Day.

Important Rules & Regulations

  • Campaigning within 10 feet of a polling place is prohibited.
